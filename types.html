Belgian Blond

This beer is brewed with Styrian and Saaz hop, which gives the beer a light character. Multiple types of malt are used, among which Abbey mout. The beer contains 5,5% Alcohol. Some walnut flavours are added which gives the beer its distinguisble taste.

IPA

Our IPA (India Pale Ale) consists of Cara and Pale malt, and Chinook hop which makes it taste fruity but bitter. The typical IPA taste, which comes from the extra hop and sugar added to the 'plain' recipy, is very present in this beer. The beer has a nice red-orange colour and contains 7% percent Alcohol.
